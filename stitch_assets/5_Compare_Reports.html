Base: 2023 Q3 Filing Filed: Jul 28, 2023

Item 2. Management’s Discussion and Analysis

Total net sales increased 2% to $81.8 billion during the third quarter of 2023, compared to the same quarter in 2022. This was primarily driven by higher Services net sales, partially offset by lower iPhone and Mac net sales.

The Company expects macroeconomic factors, including inflation and foreign exchange fluctuations, to continue to impact results in future periods.

"Supply chain constraints have eased significantly since the prior year, allowing for normalization of inventory levels across major product lines."

Item 2. Management’s Discussion and Analysis

Total net sales increased 5% to $85.8 billion during the third quarter of 2024, compared to the same quarter in 2023. This was primarily driven by higher Services net sales, and strong performance in iPad and Wearables segments.

The Company expects macroeconomic factors, particularly relating to global trade tensions and evolving regulatory requirements in the EU, to continue to impact results in future periods.

"Supply chain operations remain stable; however, we are actively diversifying manufacturing hubs to mitigate geopolitical risks and ensure long-term resilience."
